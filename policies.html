Cancellation & Refund Policy

Quick summary:
  • Cancel a request before the coach approves: full refund (your card was only authorized, not charged)
  • Cancel more than 24 hours before a confirmed lesson: full refund
  • Cancel less than 24 hours before a confirmed lesson: no refund
  • Coach cancels a confirmed lesson: full refund, always

For students

When you book a lesson, your card is authorized for the full amount but not charged immediately. You're only charged once the coach confirms the booking. You can cancel a pending request anytime before the coach approves it, with no charge.

Once a lesson is confirmed and your card has been charged, you can still cancel — but the refund depends on timing:

  • More than 24 hours before the lesson: You receive a full refund. Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days.
  • Within 24 hours of the lesson: Cancellations are not refundable. This protects coaches who have set aside time for your lesson.

If the coach cancels for any reason, you receive a full refund regardless of timing.

If a coach doesn't respond

Coaches have 24 hours to confirm or decline a booking request. If a coach doesn't respond within 24 hours, the request is automatically cancelled and your card authorization is released — no charge is made.

Disputes & special circumstances

If you experience an issue with a lesson (no-show, safety concern, dissatisfaction), email us at team@swingablegolf.com within 48 hours of the lesson. We review disputes case-by-case.

How Swingable Golf Works

Swingable Golf is a marketplace that connects students with independent golf coaches. We do not employ coaches and do not provide golf instruction directly.

  • Coaches are independent contractors, not employees of Swingable Golf
  • Each coach sets their own rates, availability, and lesson logistics
  • Swingable Golf provides the platform, processes payments, and handles transactional support
  • The relationship between students and coaches is between those parties

Payments & Fees

What students pay

The total charge shown at checkout includes the coach's lesson rate plus an 8% service fee. Both are clearly itemized before payment.

How coaches get paid

Coaches receive 92% of the lesson rate they set. Stripe processes payouts directly to the coach's bank account, typically within 2 business days after the lesson date.

Tax forms

Stripe issues 1099-K forms to coaches earning more than $600 per year on Swingable Golf. Coaches are responsible for their own tax reporting on lesson income.

Disputes & chargebacks

If a student disputes a charge with their bank, we cooperate fully with both parties to resolve. Frivolous chargebacks may result in account suspension.
