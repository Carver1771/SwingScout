Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before booking
How much does a lesson cost?▾
Prices range from $90 to $150/hr depending on the instructor. Each sets their own rate — displayed clearly on their profile.
Do I need my own clubs?▾
Not necessarily. Many instructors have loaners, and driving ranges like Topgolf provide clubs for free. Ask your instructor when booking.
How does booking work?▾
Submit a request with your preferred time and skill level. Your instructor reaches out within 24 hours to confirm details and schedule your lesson.
Can I cancel or reschedule?▾
Yes — coordinate directly with your instructor. Most ask for 24 hours notice. Their policy is shared when they confirm your booking.
How much does Swingable Golf cost?▾
Browsing instructor profiles, reading reviews, and submitting lesson requests is available to all students. Pricing for any additional services will always be clearly communicated upfront.
